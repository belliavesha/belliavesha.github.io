Magnetically-dominated plasma turbulence in the rapid synchrotron-cooling regime

An "award-winning" poster on magnetized plasma turbulence simulations. We simulate the extreme regime, where synchrotron cooling time is shorter than plasma timescales. We find that the turbulence is not suppressed in this regime and sustains non-thermal particle acceleration.

HEPRO VIII, Paris, France

29-31 October 2023

Efficient Polarimetric Analysis of Accretion Disks around Kerr Black Holes

Here I focus on efficient polarimetric analysis of Kerr black hole accretion disks, utilising data from IXPE. Our efficient analytical method, based on the Schwarzschild metric, rapidly deduces essential black hole parameters while bypassing complex numerical techniques.

Polarized x-rays constrain the geometry in the black hole X-ray binaries

Here I briefly explain how we learn about the geometries of black hole X-ray binaries, and Cyg X-1 in particular.
