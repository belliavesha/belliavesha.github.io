Efficient Polarimetric Analysis of Accretion Disks around Kerr Black Holes

Here I focus on efficient polarimetric analysis of Kerr black hole accretion disks, utilising data from IXPE. Our efficient analytical method, based on the Schwarzschild metric, rapidly deduces essential black hole parameters while bypassing complex numerical techniques.
